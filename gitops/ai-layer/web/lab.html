🌯 Open BurritoBot →

This cluster is yours. 🧙

You watched BurritoBot burn down a shared cluster in rounds 1 and 2. Now you have your own. The shell on the right is already wired to it. Work down the steps. Tap any command to copy it, then paste into the terminal.

1
Look around your cluster

Your kubectl is already pointed at your cluster. No setup. See what is running:

kubectl get pods -A

The agent, its guard-proxy, and BurritoBot all live in the agent namespace.

2
Order a burrito from your BurritoBot

Open your storefront and place an order. It is the same witch you met in rounds 1 and 2, now running on your cluster.

Open BurritoBot → the “Open BurritoBot” button, top right

Order all five steps (protein, base, fillings, salsa, toppings) and see what she says at checkout. 🌯

3
The guardrails are yours to flip

In round 1 nothing was watching the agent. On your cluster, you control the AI guardrails. Turn them off, poke at BurritoBot, then turn them back on and try the same thing:

guards-off
guards-on
Run a command, then re-order or re-ask in BurritoBot and watch the difference. With guards on, the proxy steps in. With guards off, you are talking straight to the model.
4
Watch it in Datadog

Everything the agent does on your cluster shows up in Datadog: the prompts, the token cost, the traces. Keep your Datadog tab open beside this one and watch the cost tick as you order.

Your Datadog login is on the page where you claimed this cluster. The API/APP keys are already wired into your cluster's agent.
5
Try to make it burn (optional)

This is your cluster, so break whatever you like. See if you can get BurritoBot to do something it shouldn't with the guards off, then turn them on and watch it get stopped.

Some damage is walled off at the infrastructure level no matter what you do, that is the point of the lab. If you wreck something past repair, you have your own cluster, so no harm done to anyone else.

Lost? The terminal on the right is the source of truth. Re-run kubectl get pods -A any time to confirm you are still connected.

On a small screen the terminal is below these steps. A laptop is the better experience.
